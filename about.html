The Studio7

Through the experiences of many years we provide the best service for you with professional digital equipment and latest technology. We have created unique and colorful albums and video for our clients. Our goal is to provide you not just a photo album or a video of your event, but an irreplaceable piece of memory that emphasizes the beauty and the uniqueness of your day.

Mission & Vision

To capture, imprint and preserve forever, the most cherished memories of the special moments of your life. Our passion is to give you an ultimate satisfaction in retracing those memories & emotions, with our commitment and excellence to be the Best Wedding Photographer & Videograper in Sri lanka.
